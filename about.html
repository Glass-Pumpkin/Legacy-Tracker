About

Hello! Thank you for visiting my page! I'm Serena, and I'm a collector of discontinued items in FFXIV. I primarily collect items from the 1.x version of this game, but there are a handful of 2.x things I actively collect as well.

On the "Items" page, you'll find an interactive visual list of the items I'm currently collecting. Clicking on any icon will bring up a small window displaying that item's name and my current total. You can click off anywhere to remove this window afterwards. Grey icons are items that I do not own. If you have any of these, I'd be very interested in hearing from you!

Are you a collector as well? Do you have something I'm after and vice versa? Please do send me a message! I'm always open to trades with fellow collectors. Let's help each other complete our collections!
